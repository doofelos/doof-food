what is a cheese quesadilla?!

a cheese quesadilla is a tortilla (or two!) filled with cheese and toasted. esta muy muy delicioso!



griddies

  • tortilla
  • cheese
  • sour cream

stipsies

  1. heat up pan
  2. place tortilla and top with cheese
  3. toast tortilla until cheese melted
  4. take tortilla off pan and cut into fourths
  5. top with sour cream and serve and enjoy!!!!
